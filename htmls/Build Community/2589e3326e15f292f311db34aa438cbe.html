90-Year-Old Woman Gives Back to Community

Posted: Sep 18, 2016 06:56 PM CDT

Updated: Sep 18, 2016 06:56 PM CDT

WALCOTT, Ark.-- A 90-year-old woman is celebrating her birthday by giving back to her community.

"Her dedication and her love for others is certainly evidence of what she chose to do for her birthday!" said a family friend.

Catty Adams' family says Catty has always wanted to do something for the Mt. Zion Cemetery in Walcott Arkansas.

Community members and family got together Sunday to help the woman hold a fundraiser for the cemetery.

Adams says it's  filled with history and it's important to keep it beautiful.

"It means everything. People have lost all interest in the cemetery and when the young people don't come back they think the old people aught to do the work. Well it is time for the young people to get off the board and go to work," said Adams.

Adams says she has family members buried in the cemetery and it's been on her heart to improve it.

The family has raised more than seven-thousand dollars to go towards those improvements.
