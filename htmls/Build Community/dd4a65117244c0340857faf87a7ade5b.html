Learn four simple ways you can give back to your community and show your appreciation for others and make an impact on someone's life. For more videos, subscribe to the Kin Community YouTube channel: http://bit.ly/BlogSubs

4 Ways To Give Back To Your Community

1. Give the gift of laughter to a children's hospital.

Care packages are one of the best ways to show your love and support. Click here to learn how to make a fun-filled care package that will brighten a child's day!

 

2. Donate clothes to a shelter.

Do you have clothes taking up space in your closet? Give your closet an overhaul and donate gently used clothing and accessories to your local shelter. Put those extra sweaters or hats you have laying around your apartment to good use!

 

3. Throw a bake sale to raise money for charity.

Bake sales are an easy and delicious way to fundraise and help support a cause that's near and dear to your heart. Wrap your delicious baked goods in custom packaging! Click here for a variety of bake sale packaging that you can make at home.

 

4. Write a thank you letter to a public officer.

Let your community leaders know how much you appreciate all of the hard work they do. Skip the stationary store and make customized paper envelope liners that are sure to make an impression.
