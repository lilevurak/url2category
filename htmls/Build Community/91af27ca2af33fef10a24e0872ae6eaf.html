Ohio State football players give back by doing community service at LifeCare Alliance

Ohio State football players give back by doing community service at LifeCare Alliance
Ohio State football players give back by doing community service at LifeCare Alliance | WBNS-10TV Columbus, Ohio | Columbus News, Weather & Sports
10TV Web Staff
Published: 11/23/16 09:58 pm EST
Updated: 12/11/16 10:55 pm EST
COLUMBUS, Ohio

Just days ahead of The Game against Michigan, Ohio State spent time giving back.

Members of the football team packed boxes of food and made plates for Meals On Wheels at the two LifeCare Alliance facilities in central Ohio.

This is the fourth consecutive year the Buckeyes have pitched in at either LifeCare Alliance or the Mid-Ohio Food Bank.
