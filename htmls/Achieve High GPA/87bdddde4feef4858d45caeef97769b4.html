Do STRAIGHT F's, no SAT/ACT scores, no high school diploma get you into the TOP COLLEGES?

Share this Video:
https://youtu.be/qD2FtoLhHIY

Subscribe To My Channel and Get More Great Tips:
https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCiwT...

As a high school student, I earned STRAIGHT F's, NEVER took my SATs and dropped out never completing my high school diploma or GED.

In this inspirational video, I"m going to prove to you that getting accepted to the TOP COLLEGES does solely depend on your high school performance, it all depends on your PASSION! You only need one person to believe in you and that is yourself...

I was eventually accepted into the University of California, Berkeley and earned my Master's in Business Administration from the University of Michigan. Today, there is overwhelming pressure on students to excel and get accepted to the top colleges. Any misstep can seem catastrophic for them.

My mission in life is to share my story in the hopes that it will provide students with some hope and guidance during some of the rough patches that lay ahead.
