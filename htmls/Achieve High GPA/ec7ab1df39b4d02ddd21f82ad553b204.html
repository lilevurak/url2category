Published on Dec 26, 2015

Demystifying College Admissions: If I haven't done well in school the past couple of years, is there anything I can do now to help me get into a good college? Does my extracurricular involvement need to be continuous all through high school, or will a year or two suffice? And if I applied to some colleges Early Action and to some with Regular Decision deadlines, will I have time to decide between the two before committing? If you're asking yourself any of these college admissions questions, Dakotah will help demystify them in this week's video!

Dakotah Eddy is the Assistant Director of Admissions Consulting for Veritas Prep and helps support both consultants and clients alike. At her double undergraduate and MBA alma mater Cornell, Dakotah served as an Admissions Ambassador, Park Leadership Fellow and Consortium member. She has been helping applicants pursue their academic dreams since she was an undergraduate, and hopes to help high schoolers demystify the idea of applying to college.
