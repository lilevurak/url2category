Published on Oct 11, 2014

Just a very quick video on how you can make a simple yet effective study timetable in order to stay on top of your semester and have the success you desire!

Doing well at school/university is largely dependent on your ability to stay organised and get things done on time. By implementing a study timetable into your daily routine you will be able to make the most of the present time and plan for the future, thus aiding your ability to achieve the academic success you desire.
