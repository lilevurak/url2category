What is ASC?

The Academic Skills Center (ASC) offers a variety of academic enrichment programs consisting of Supplemental Workshops and Study Session in addition to online study strategies available 24 hours a day.

Supplemental Workshops in Math

The Cal Poly Supplemental Workshops in Math is an intensive mathematics program for students who are enrolled in mathematics courses from MATH 116 to 244.

Continue Reading >

Supplemental Workshops in Science

Supplemental Workshops in Science provide an accelerated learning environment for selected classes. Guided by trained facilitators, students participate in discussions, compare note, develop study strategies and challenge their understanding with practice

Study Strategies Library

The Academic Skills Center is committed to guiding students with their studies; achieving synergy with work, school, and life outside of school; and much more. In this endeaver, we have accumulated a collection of strategies we think will guide students to thrive at Cal Poly.

Study Session

Study Session forms study groups for students who submit a request to study with a group focused on challenging, selected courses in the Colleges of Science and Math and the College of Engineering.
