How to find life purpose, is it a life calling or a career? What is life purpose? How do you how do find it life purpose? How do you stay motivated, fulfilled, and moving toward your dreams?

Life purpose is defined as who you are, why you were born, and who you are meant to be; it is your life's calling. Everyone is born with unique talents, gifts, and natural abilities.

Make it your mission to use your talents, gifts, and natural abilities to serve others with what you love to do! This is what brings true satisfaction and joy giving life all new meaning!

Use the Life Purpose planning forms to plan, organize, and complete your life purpose statement.

STEPS TO FIND PURPOSE IN LIFE

How can young people find their life purpose and plan? Many young people generally lack a sense of purpose and direction in their lives. They are not sure what they want to do with their life, and they are not sure that they would be successful if they knew what they wanted to do. How can young people find their life purpose and plan? Is it simply choosing a vocation and working really hard to be successful? Is success just making money and becoming rich and famous?

You need to believe that you are special and that you were born for a purpose. You have natural talents and gifts that are unique to you.

Your parents, nationality, birth order, ability to learn, disabilities, what you look like (height, bone structure, hair, eyes, skin, nose, ears, etc.), and your birth were not an accident or mistake, no matter the circumstances.

Obstacles are good! They build character, making you stronger and better. You cannot change your past, your family, and circumstances that are out of your control, but you have a choice to make a decision to change your future. You were born for a special purpose, plan, and mission!

This decision to change your future is a choice that involves some difficult, but not impossible steps. Many times young people believe lies about themselves and they have destructive painful memories that become roadblocks and obstacles that stop them. These lies and painful memories keep them from finding their life purpose, a dream, and becoming the beautiful person they were meant to be.

PURPOSE IN LIFE AND SERVICE

Purpose in life comes when you get involved and invest in the real needs of people. You can experience the joy that comes from serving others. You need role models that demonstrate moral courage and character qualities regardless of the circumstances or consequences. When you discover and pursue your life purpose it provides a motivation to establish life goals, to make wise decisions, to discover who you are, and experience fulfillment, joy, and satisfaction.
