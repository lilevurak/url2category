                                                    The Graduate Life Center

                                                    The hub of graduate activity on campus, the Graduate Life Center, known as the GLC, provides spaces for meetings, classes, social gatherings, down time, and housing close to Downtown Blacksburg.

                                                    It's also home to the Graduate School's administrative offices.
