Admissions

At The University of Akron, the possibilities are unlimited.

Choose from more than 200 undergraduate majors and areas of study. UA also has more than 100 master’s and doctoral programs, a law degree with four tracks and a pathway to a medical degree at Northeast Ohio Medical University.

On this site, learn more about the breadth and quality of the academic programs, vibrant campus life, opportunities for real-world experience and much more.
