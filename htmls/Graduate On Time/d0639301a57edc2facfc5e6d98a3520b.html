~PLEASE SUBSCRIBE!!!! ~

So once my 11th grade was over, I received my high school diploma and instead of spending my last year in school like I was supposed to, I actually got the chance to travel to Tokyo for 3 months and Seoul for 1 month and then I spent the rest of the year working and saving more money for bigger projects. A lot of the people I spoke to thought that I must have been some kid genius or something

....and who knows... maybe I am haha ;P

but me graduating high school has less to do with the fact that I'm a genius and more to do with my passion for travel and how that drove me to plan my high school career. So here are some of the tips I used to graduate high school early and I hope it helps you in someway!
