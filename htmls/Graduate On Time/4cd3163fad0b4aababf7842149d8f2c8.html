A full course load is 12-15 hours (4 or 5 classes) and that stresses college students out enough. So yeah you can do it. but you'll kill yourself doing it. Online classes are much, MUCH harder.
Also, nowadays people gennerally finish in 5 years instead of 4. I would reccomend just going for 4 or five years. If you wanna just get out there and start your life look into trade school.
