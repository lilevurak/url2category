Application for Graduation

An Application for Graduation must be submitted early in the semester of expected graduation. Seniors must adhere to a graduation schedule prepared each year by the College Office. A late fee will be charged for failure to apply for graduation by the date specified. Check the application timeline for May, August and December graduation.
