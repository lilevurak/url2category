Find my career as a Christian - Learn your career as a Christian today

To learn more visit http://masters.life

Are you looking for something more? You may be surprised to know that this is Gods design. If you retired from your job, what would you retire to? Our day job is an important part of our lives, one that we may never grow out of, but calling is what you were made for. Do you have clarity about the difference? There's something in ingrained in God's design for our culture and our life that leans towards celebrating productivity. The Bible tells us how we can learn from the life of Paul. How his career funded his ministry and calling. Your professional activities can provide opportunity to enhance Gods public image through your work and efforts. Do it all for the glory of God. As a Christian, our influence starts in the marketplace but it doesn't end there. Let's impact the world in preparation for eternity. Ponder these quotes as Bob Shank of the Masters Program gives direction on calling vs career.

-"Career is what you're paid for, calling is what youre made for

-There's a difference between what you're hired to do, and what you're wired to do

-"Your career will probably be celebrated here, you're calling will definitely be celebrated there

The Masters Program Online is a mentoring experience designed for Christians who want their leadership potential - over their lifetime - to be maximized. Masters Online helps Christian leaders explore, expose and exploit their Kingdom Calling, while refining every aspect of their personal, family and professional friendships. This is the truest, most powerful lifestyle for work life balance.

Who is The Masters Program for?
TMP is for men and women who know they were made for more than their current experience, and are looking for a mentoring experience that will help them get clear about their unique path going forward.

We welcome you to join the growing community in our online mentorship
