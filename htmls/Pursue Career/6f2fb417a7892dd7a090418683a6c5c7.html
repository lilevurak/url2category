Should I Put Travel Above My Career? Travel or career? The answer is easy. Many people believe the only way to gain skills for getting a job is by working or education! That's wrong...taking a gap year, studying abroad, work and travel, all this can help you to improve the skills you need for your job. In this video I will show you which skills we have improved while travelling the world for 9 months. Skills you can even mention in your job interview. So don't be afraid... Should I go or should I stay? Yes, go and explore the world!
