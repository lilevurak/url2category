http://resumesthatresumecareers.com Don Burrows career in Human Resources spanned three decades. For 15 of those 30 years, Don was a recruiter for my Fortune 500 employers. For all 30 years, Don has been writing functional rsums that helped hundreds of job seekers stand out and shine.




Whether you are unemployed and looking for work or a solopreneur looking for customers, the people you want to talk with are primarily concerned with what you have accomplished.
Don can teach you how to present yourself as the "ideal" candidate or vendor.
Don put this knowledge into his book, Resumes That Resume Careers. "Don is a true professional career coach."
