Student Success Center: Overview

Augie Opportunities is Augustana's centralized job and internship database.

Students and Alumni | Employers

Students and Alumni

Access your account: Student Login | Alumni Login.

Login Information for Students: We've made it easy for you by creating your account. Your login is your Augustana email and your student ID number serves as your password. You may also view a listing of the jobs and internships advertised within the system through a weekly report published on the portal (login required.)

Login Information for Alumni: If you have NOT registered with this site before and graduated during the last five years, your Augustana email address is your login and your student ID number serves as your password.  For alumni who cannot remember their AugieLink ID number, it's listed within the bottom box of your monthly alumni enews. You may also use the "forgot your password" feature below the login box or contact us for assistance. If you graduated more than five years ago, please create an account. Once we verify your alumni status, you will have access to the system.

Why should you use your account?

  • This system grants you access to 2,000+ jobs and internships posted annually through the Student Success Center.
  • It is FREE and accessible through any mobile device, PC, or Mac 24/7, 365 days a year.
  • You can publish multiple resumes for employers who are interested in Augustana students and alumni.
  • When you find positions that interest you, you can interface with your LinkedIn account so you can identify who you know at organizations before you apply.
  • You'll gain access to the Student Success Center’s Career Resource Library — exclusively available only to Augustana students and alumni.
  • If that's not enough, you will be able to access even more opportunities published by Indeed.com, College Recruiter, and Internships.com.
  • Plus, you can receive automatic updates of positions that match your search criteria and stay informed of Success Center announcements via email or text (whichever you prefer).

Employers

Advertising positions with us is simple. Choose from the following options:

  • Create an Augie Opportunities account. This permits you to receive reminders when your posting is about to expire and to browse the resumes published by both our students and alumni. We've designed the online form to be easy, and once approved, both current students and alumni will be able to view your positions at no cost.
  • Share your position with us and we will post it for you for free. Simply forward the details to success@augie.edu or 605.274.5293 (Fax). 

We are eager to answer any of your questions and support you in your recruitment. Please contact us at success@augie.edu or 605.274.4127.
