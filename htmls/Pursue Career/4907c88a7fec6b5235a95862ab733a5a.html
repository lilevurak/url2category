Looking for a job or internship? Need help with your resume or cover letter? The Career Center is a great place to start! While we won't place you in a specific job, we can help you along every step of the way.

Looking for a job?

Find jobs resources using the links here or you can come into the Career Center to find even more job listings or meet with a professional who can help you strengthen your resume, cover letter, or job interviewing skills.

Looking for an internship?

In addition to building practical job skills, an internship can benefit you in many ways. Find out how and use our list of online resources to help find the right internship for you!

Need help with your resume?

The benefits of having a resume are many and most jobs require a one, so it is a good idea to have one prepared. Here we list the many benefits of having a well-written resume and how to get started. We can help!

Need interviewing tips?

Your resume can get you an interview, but the interview is what will get you the job. Do you know how to best prepare for interviews? What is appropriate to wear? What kinds of questions to expect? We have answers to your questions!

Have Questions?

Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions about searching for a job.
