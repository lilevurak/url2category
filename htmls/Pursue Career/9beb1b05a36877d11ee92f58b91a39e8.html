                  This guide is for all Virginia Tech students: 

                  • Undergraduates: first year through senior level 
                  • Graduate students 
                  • Students of all majors 
                  • Seeking experience during college, including internships 
                  • Seeking post-graduation jobs 

                  This guide covers starting out to deciding on a job offer. 

                  • Begin by being your best self with integrity, honesty, and professional conduct.
                  • Effective search strategies can vary by career field. Begin with where do I start looking?
                  • Presenting yourself includes much more than just your resume. 
                  • Don't forget about your online identity. 

                  Along your journey, we can help you tailor your strategies to your career field and goals through advising. Your goals are unique to you, and it can also be informative to see the first jobs and employers of VT grads.
