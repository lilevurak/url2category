How to Find a Career That Fulfills Your True Purpose

Aired on 03/02/2014 | tv-14
Ivy, 26, has been trying to find a career path that merges her inner and outer purposes but isn't having much luck. Now, she has two questions for spiritual author Eckhart Tolle: How can she find a career that is fulfilling but doesn't strengthen her ego? And how can she embrace the uncertainty in her life right now? Watch as Eckhart offers his guidance.
