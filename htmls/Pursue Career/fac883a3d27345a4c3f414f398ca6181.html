Use transferable skills to help you get an interview and get a job. It can be difficult to find examples of skills for your CV or resume if you are a school leaver or have just left college. The same applies if you are going back to work after bringing up your children or after a break for any other reason. But even though you might not have recent work experience, you have plenty of life experience and as a result, plenty of skills which will help you in the workplace. these skills can really enhance your CV or resume and prove to employers that you have the skills needed for the job.
