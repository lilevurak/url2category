How To Find Balance In A Busy Life?

Nowadays, people seem to be caught up in a never-ending busy life. We have a lot of stuff to do, which includes a full-time job, education, commute time, leisure time, gym, taking care of our sons & daughters, etc.

So, how do you find balance in such a busy life? How should you approach your routine to make sure you will get your stuff done in time, will not neglect anything from your current week and life everything you can?

While there might be some theories about this which are meant to create complex systems, everything comes down to discipline and dividing your time in a way that you can make sure you will get everything in your schedule.

In this video I will present you with some good techniques and also a new concept which might be a little bit different from what you know now.
