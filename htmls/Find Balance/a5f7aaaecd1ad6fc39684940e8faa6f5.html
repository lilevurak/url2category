How to Find Balance | Top 5 Tips For Balancing Your Life
S U B S C R I B E : https://www.youtube.com/user/RafaelEl...
Want to work with me? http://www.rafaeleliassen.com/

Want balance in life? It's rather simple. Find out what truly matters to you. Tip number one is to figure out your priorities, where should most of your time be spent? If you can figure this out everything will become much easier when it comes to actually balancing your life.

Also, don't buy into this modern day narrative of balance, it's nonsense. You should make your life what you want it to be, and not according to some story you've been sold by your family, friends or the movies. I mean really, what do you want? That's all that matters.

Listen to your soul, your heart, it already knows what you need to do, and balancing is much easier with a clear mind. Less thoughts = better. Meditation is a great way to free up your mind so you can really get in touch with what's going on within. Balancing your mind can be a great place to start to achieve ultimate balance.
