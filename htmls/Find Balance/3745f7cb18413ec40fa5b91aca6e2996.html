Published on Apr 3, 2017

Do you feel tired, overworked or like you have too much to do? These can be signs of stress and you may have talked about it with your friends and coworkers. Stress is a common part of the modern lifestyle, but did you know that it can impact your physical, mental and spiritual health?

In this interactive webinar, we will go over a powerful and effective technique based on introspection and self-analysis that allows us to understand and overcome stress from the root. Join us to learn more about this technique and how to overcome stress.

About your host: Radha started her spiritual practice with SSRF in 2009 and is from Vancouver, Canada. She is an Early Childhood Educator by profession. Her service includes organizing SSRF Webinars and she is responsible for all activities and seekers in Canada. She is also conducting SSRF workshops in Canada & India.
