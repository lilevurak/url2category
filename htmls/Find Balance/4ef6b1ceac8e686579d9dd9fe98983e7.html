Published on Nov 29, 2011

http://thedailylockerroom.com To lead a happy life, you must find a balance between your leisure time and your work. Those who play too much are rarely successful in their careers, and those who work too much can miss out on time with friends, family and seeing the world.

Breaks allow us the time to recharge and help us think of creative ways to solve problems. They also give us the time to enjoy outings with the ones we love and experience our world. After a break, you'll come back better equipped to work. Even in these lean times, remember to strive for a healthy work / life balance.

Plan time to exercise and turn your phone off when you do. Set time aside each day to speak with and spend time (if possible) with your family. When you work, work! When you are off, enjoy your time off. Stop thinking about work when you are off and playing when you are working.

Balance is the key to living your very best life. It demands that you look at how you spend your days and decide that you will fit in each day those things that are important and meaningful to you so you can live a balanced and exciting life. http://thedailylockerroom.com
