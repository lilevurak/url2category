Having stress at work? Having stress at home? Having stress over money problems? Stress in studies or even feel stress when everything does not go smooth for you?

Stress is part of our life and I believed that everyone will face stress at certain stages in your life. The most common causes of stress include major life changes, financial problems, work, relationship difficulties, perfectionism and others. In addition, the causes of stress leads to effects such as depression, heart diseases and others. Certain people can control or adapt to stress but not all of them can manage or overcome it.
