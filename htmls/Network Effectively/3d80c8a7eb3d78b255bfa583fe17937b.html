  • Full-Time MBA

    An intensive, on-campus opportunity to drive career change and growth. Customized curriculum and concentrations with a rich blend of rigorous studies and ample out-of-class leadership development experiences. Learn more >

  • Executive MBA Programs

    Offered in three formats: Evening MBA, Weekend MBA & Global OneMBA. The Executive MBA Programs allow ambitious professionals to earn an MBA and reach professional goals without life interruption. Learn more >

  • Online MBA@UNC

    The innovative MBA@UNC gives working professionals the opportunity to advance careers from anywhere in the world. The same world-class faculty, rigorous curriculum and top-caliber student community of our on-campus programs, offered in a way that fits your life. Learn more >

  • Executive Development

    A portfolio of non-degree programs designed to help individuals become better, more effective business leaders. We also partner with organizations to develop their top talent with programs that are customized to address specific business challenges. Learn more >

  • PhD Program

    Our top-ranked doctorate program with six areas of concentration position you with a range of career options. Nationally renowned faculty provide the skills and strategies to produce creative and significant research. Financial support available. Learn more >

  • Master of Accounting

    Pursue a Master of Accounting degree from UNC Kenan-Flagler and youll position yourself for an outstanding career in accounting. Youll see extraordinary benefits now and in years to come. Come from any undergraduate background and get recruited by top firms, earn a strong salary, and put the power of the UNC alumni network to work for you. One year. A lifetime of R.O.I. Learn more >

  • Undergraduate Business

    Two-year program offering students a rigorous, functional and versatile curriculum with practical applications. Our focus on leadership, applied learning and real-world experience, as well as professional and career development, guide students toward successful and satisfying careers. Learn more >

  • UNC Business Essentials

    This online business certificate program provides students and early career professionals with a solid foundation in the fundamentals of business. The innovative technology offers you the flexibility to work at your own pace, anytime, from anywhere in the world. Learn more >

Institute & Centers

  • The Kenan Institute

    Finding ways to connect people and organizations, create opportunities and resources, and accelerate the achievements of our partners. We help innovative entrepreneurs, support North Carolina economic development, and build bridges with other institutions. Learn more >

  • Hodges Leadership Center

    The Luther H. Hodges Leadership Center supports leadership education. Our mission is to develop our students to become exceptional leaders who positively impact the organizations they lead and the communities they serve. Learn more >

  • Sustainable Enterprise

    Through our world-class curriculum, real-life student experiences and relevant research, the Center for Sustainable Enterprise develops leaders for the world we envision using business to innovate for global change. Learn more >

  • Real Estate

    The Leonard W. Wood Center for Real Estate Studies exists to enrich the real estate experience at UNC Kenan-Flagler. The Wood Center is responsible for a number of initiatives that together yield innovative programming, invested faculty and staff and engaged alumni. Learn more >

  • Entrepreneurial Studies

    Students receive the resources to identify and evaluate entrepreneurial opportunities through acclaimed programs such as Launching the Venture, Carolina Venture Fellows and the student-run KFBS Private Equity Fund. Learn more >

  • Global Business Center

    The Global Business Center's research and educational programs teach students and companies to conduct business around the world and educators to prepare them for it. Learn more >

  • Family Enterprise

    Creating next generation leaders who are prepared to lead their family enterprises, maintain healthy family and working relationships, and be responsible stewards of family business assets. Learn more >
