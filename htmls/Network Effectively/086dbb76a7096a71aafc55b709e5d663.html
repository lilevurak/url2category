Making Teaching Better

We are an independent non-profit organisation committed to world-class teaching in higher education. We work in partnership with institutions and individuals in higher education supporting student success. The Higher Education Academy brings the sector together to collaborate, and share teaching strategies and practice. Learn more about HEA >
