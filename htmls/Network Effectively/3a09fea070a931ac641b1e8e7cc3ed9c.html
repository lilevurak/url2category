Faculty Teaching Initiatives

Take advantage of multiple resources developed for Yale Faculty members to make teaching and learning more public and collaborative, so that every Yale instructor experiences the satisfaction that results from teaching well.

Graduate Student, Professional Student, and Postdoctoral Teaching Development

The CTL offers a full range of training, consultation, and teacher development services to postdoctoral and graduate fellows. This includes our workshop series, teaching opportunities, the Certificate of College Teaching Preparation, and information about preparing for the academic job market.

STEM Education and Evaluation

The CTL offers a full range of services to STEM instructors, including a Scientific Teaching Fellows course for graduate students and post-docs, Summer Institutes for STEM faculty and future faculty, a STEM Education Seminar Series, and instructional materials.

Instructional Consultations & Classroom Observations

The Center for Teaching and Learning routinely assists members of the Yale community with individual instructional consultations and classroom observations.

Funding Opportunities

Throughout the academic year, the CTL provides funding for various projects for faculty, postdoctoral fellows/associates, and graduate and professional students. The CTL also curates a brief list of other funding opportunities available on- and off-campus.

Teach in Our Space

The CTL features several spaces designed to accommodate courses and other teaching and learning activities.
