This video gives you 10 different ways to save a few coins.
From re-using supermarket carrier bags to finding local free wifi spots.
Hopefully these tips help you out.
Feel free to write more tips in the comments below.

10 Quick money saving tips for students:

1. Cut open bottles and tubes to get the last little bit out
2. Loyalty cards - get stamps to gain a free meal
- Supermarket cards (countdown) - swipe to gain points
3. Using free wifi around your town or city
4. Free haircuts - be a hair model for local trainee hairdressers
5. Use plastic supermarket bags instead of buying specialist bags for bin liners
6. Use supermarket bags to wrap up your lunch
7. Re-use tea bags
8. Hang up washing to dry - either outside or around your home / room (make sure to open the window)
9. Put water in last bit of washing up liquid to prolong useage
10. Buy a flask instead of always buying water bottles. Fill up at the tap or using water coolers in schools, gyms, offices or some shops or restaurants.
